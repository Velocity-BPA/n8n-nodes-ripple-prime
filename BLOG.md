# Automate Institutional Trading with Our New Ripple Prime Node for n8n

We're excited to announce the release of our latest community node: n8n-nodes-ripple-prime, bringing institutional-grade prime brokerage and multi-asset trading capabilities directly into your n8n workflows.

Institutional traders and fintech companies working with Ripple Prime face a common challenge: integrating prime brokerage services into their operational workflows often requires extensive custom development. Whether you're managing multi-asset portfolios, executing trades across various instruments, or reconciling brokerage data with internal systems, the manual coding and API integration work can be time-consuming and error-prone.

## Streamlined Automation for Prime Brokerage

The n8n-nodes-ripple-prime node eliminates this friction by bringing Ripple Prime's institutional trading capabilities into the visual, no-code environment of n8n. Now you can build sophisticated trading workflows, connect prime brokerage operations with your existing tools, and automate complex multi-asset trading strategies—all without writing a single line of code.

- **Multi-Asset Trading Support**: Execute and manage trades across various asset classes through Ripple Prime's institutional infrastructure
- **Prime Brokerage Integration**: Access comprehensive prime brokerage services directly from your n8n workflows
- **Seamless Connectivity**: Connect Ripple Prime with thousands of other apps and services available in the n8n ecosystem

- **Enterprise-Ready**: Designed for institutional requirements with robust error handling and reliable execution

## Getting Started

Installing the Ripple Prime node is simple. In your n8n instance, run:


npm install n8n-nodes-ripple-prime


After installation, restart n8n and you'll find the Ripple Prime node available in your node palette, ready to integrate into your workflows.
